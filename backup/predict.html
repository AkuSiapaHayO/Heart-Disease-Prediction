Heart Disease Prediction Questionnaire

Welcome! Please take a few moments to answer the following questions. Each one is designed to help assess your risk for heart disease. Detailed explanations are provided to assist you in giving accurate answers.

Personal Information



Select the range that best represents your age.




Provide your height in meters. For example, 1.75 meters.




Provide your weight in kilograms for accurate BMI calculations. For example, 70.5 kg.




Enter your Body Mass Index (BMI). This is a measure of your weight in relation to your height. If unsure, you can calculate it by dividing your weight (kg) by the square of your height (m).



Physical and General Health

Provide an overall assessment of your health, based on your own understanding and experiences.




Enter the number of days in the past 30 days when your mental health was not good.




Enter the number of days in the past 30 days when your physical health was not good.




Enter the average number of hours you sleep per day. Aim for accuracy for the best prediction.



Medical History

Arthritis refers to inflammation of the joints that may cause pain or stiffness.




COPD, or Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease, is a chronic lung condition that makes breathing difficult.




Specify if you have diabetes, pre-diabetes, or diabetes that occurred during pregnancy.




Indicate if you have been diagnosed with any chronic kidney conditions.



Physical Abilities
